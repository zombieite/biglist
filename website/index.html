WASTELAND FIREBIRD'S BIG LIST OF THE BEST THINGS ON ROUTE 66

A curious guide to Route 66 and the American Dream, updated 2026 Jul 22

This Book is available now! Click here to buy!

Wasteland Firebird's Big List of the Best Things On Route 66 is like no other Route 66 guide. It's not a map. It's a carefully curated list of addresses, updated twice yearly. It's all the best things, and nothing more. Each page has a QR code that opens up Google Maps directions to your next destination. If you stop at every address, you'll be on Route 66. No app needed! Many pages contain descriptions, reviews, recommendations, memories of previous journeys, or musings about the American Dream.

Wasteland Firebird has traveled the Route five times, adding and subtracting items from this list along the way. None of the businesses in this book paid to appear in it. This book is solely a list of places that are beautiful, old, quirky, luxurious, or delicious. Every place in this book is worth at least a brief stop. Some of the entries are new discoveries that have never before been seen in connection with Route 66, such as "Room Attack" and "Toynbee Tiles."

The previous edition of this book served as the guide for The Great Route 66 Centennial Convergence, held in April 2026. So many people asked for the book that we quickly ran out of our free copies. As soon as the trip was complete, we released this new updated edition for sale.
